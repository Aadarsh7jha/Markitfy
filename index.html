How much does a website cost? +
Website costs vary based on complexity, features, and scope. A basic business website starts around ₹15,000 while a full custom web application can go higher. We always provide a detailed, transparent quote after understanding your requirements. No hidden fees.
What services does Markitfy offer? +
We offer web design & development, mobile app development (iOS/Android), logo & brand identity, graphic design, digital & performance marketing, SEO, and business consultation. Everything under one roof.
How long does a project take? +
A standard website takes 2–4 weeks. Mobile apps take 6–12 weeks. Branding projects usually wrap in 1–2 weeks. We set clear timelines upfront and stick to them — we have a 100% on-time delivery record.
Do you offer post-launch support? +
Absolutely. We offer flexible support and maintenance packages. Whether it's bug fixes, content updates, performance optimization, or security patches — we're here after launch too.
How do you ensure quality? +
Every project goes through multiple review rounds. We involve clients throughout the process with regular check-ins. We use industry-best practices for code quality, design standards, and performance optimization.
Can you help if I already have a website? +
Yes! We handle redesigns, speed optimization, SEO improvements, and feature additions to existing websites and apps. Just share what you have and what you need — we'll take it from there.
